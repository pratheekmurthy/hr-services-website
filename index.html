About

Who we are

Sri Mahalakshmi HR Solutions was founded by Mr. Umesh Naik in Bangalore. His passion for his work shone through in his enthusiasm to do the right things for his clients. Last 2 years, Mr. Umesh Naik grew the Company to serve more than 50+ businesses. He developed and conducted in-house programs and seminars covering a wide range of HR topics, and was often a featured speaker at corporate management development programs and training sessions.And other employee issues. Additionally, HR on Demand includes a kit containing required and best practice forms, files samples, records retention guidelines, and mandatory labor law posters.


One of Sri Mahalakshmi HR Solutions greatest strengths is ability to adapt a constantly changing world.  Our flat organizational structure enables us to quickly adjust to an evolving marketplace.  Our independent minded, entrepreneurial culture excels in finding unique ways to meet your technical staffing and human resources needs.  And also we will continue to greet each day with the flexibility, creativity, and the opportunistic attitude necessary to help your business succeed.


Our consultants help you manage your regulatory compliance, policies, and procedures; recruitment and selection; employee performance management, and compensation and benefits at a cost comparable to an internal HR function. The HR consultant must also posses the skills and knowledge required to work on your particular project, a proven history of success, and willingness to partner with you and your staff to accomplish the results needed. Sri Mahalakshmi HR Solutionsconsultants are able to meet a wide variety of needs from day-to-day administrative work, to mapping out a strategic plan for your company’s long-term success.

Services

What we do offer

We offer services to various Industries

  • Biotech & Pharmaceutical company’s
  • Private Schools & Education institutes
  • Corporate Restaurants
  • Automobile Industries
  • Hospitals
  • Engineering companies
  • Transportation & Logistics Industry
  • Manufacturing industries companies etc.,

Payroll management service

  • Maintained monthly attendance register, wages register, salary slip, Preparation of ESI & P.F Challans for monthly payments and Maintenance of Records
  • Dealing with Labour Department, PF, ESI, & PT officers
  • GST Registration & Maintenance

Provident Fund (P.F)

  • Registration under P.F Act 1952
  • Preparation of P.F Challans for monthly payments
  • Maintenance of P.F Records.
  • Arranging periodicals Inspections
  • Filling of monthly/yearly returns
  • Guidance of latest rules & amendments of P.F

ESI Act

  • Registration under E.S.I.C Act 1948
  • Preparation of monthly challans for deposit of ESI contribution.
  • Maintenance of registration forms
  • Maintenance of Accident Book: filing of accident reports to ESI Authorities for claiming compensation of the injured employee during the course of employment.
  • Dealing with ESI officers.
  • Arranging periodicals Inspections
  • Maintenance of Inspection Books
  • Solving problems of employee’s workers in getting their benefit under the scheme.
  • Submission of nomination form for getting ESI Cards.
  • Submission of half yearly return with the ESI authorities.
  • Guidance to the latest amendments

Factory Act

  • Registration under Factory Act 1948
  • Registration under Factory Act/Renewal of Factory License
  • Maintenance of All Register under the Factory Act inclusive of as under:
    • Adults Registers
    • Leave with wages Registers
    • Casual & Sick leave Register
    • Accident & dangerous occurrence Registers
    • Overtime Registers
    • Fine/ Deduction Register
    • Filling of half yearly returns
    • Guidance to latest rule and amendments.

Bonus Act

  • Maintaining of Registers A, B & C
  • Arranging assessment under Bonus Act
  • Guidance to latest amendments in Bonus Act

Gratuity Act

  • Working out Gratuity of employees at the time of resignation/retirement
  • Guidance for taking up Gratuity schemes as per the requirement of the Gratuity Act
  • Payment of minimum wages Act
    • Maintenance of Registers
    • Guidance to the latest amendments and providing notification regarding changes in Minimum wages

Shops & Establishments Act and other Labour Acts

  • Registration/Renewal under Shops &; Establishment Act
  • Maintaining Statutory Registers under various Labour Act
  • Display of Notices and Abstracts
  • Interacting with Labour Department for statutory compliance

Contract Labour (R & A) Act 1970 & Central Rules 1971

  • Registration under CLRA Act
  • Maintaining Register prescribed for Principle Employer
  • Guide the Contractors in obtaining license under CLRA Act Notice of Commencement / Completion in Form VIB
  • Auditing the Records / Registers of Contractors to ensure compliance
  • Filling of monthly/yearly returns
  • Display of Notices and Abstract prescribed under CLRA Act
